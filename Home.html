ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Established in 1965 by Naoji Takahashi, Fuji Natural foods has been the leading manufacturer of natural vegetables in Japan. Fourteen years later Fuji Natural Foods, Inc. was established in Ontario, California in 1979.

At Fuji, we provide an assortment of products which include: bean sprouts, radish sprouts, pea sprouts, soybean sprouts, and tofu. We supply to a variety of vendors which include local produce markets, wholesalers and restaurants throughout Southern California and Arizona. Fuji Natural Foods Inc. has two offices located in the United States.
